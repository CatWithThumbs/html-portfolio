The Best Movies According to Aylynn

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bubble Boy

This is probably the movie that I remember most from my childhood. Fun fact: I had no idea that Jake Gyllenhal played Bubble Boy until like 5 years ago. Wild.

Matilda

Matilda is a fantastic movie and I am solemly saddended that I never unlocked my telekinesis powers after watching it. It also inspired my dream to have a beautiful garden; though I don't know how I'll fair with all the bees. Bees and I do not get along....

The SpongeBob Squarepants Movie

Spongebob holds a special place in my heart. Though I'll be 26 in two months, I'm still able to quote nearly every episode from memory. It's like I'm a sleeper agent or something.
